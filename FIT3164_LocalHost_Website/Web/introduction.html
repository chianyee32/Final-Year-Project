Cancer Drug Response Predictor

Cancer Cell Line Resistance to Drugs Prediction Tool using Multi-omics Data

For our final year project at Monash University Malaysia, our team of 4 have developed a multi-omics machine learning model to predict a cancer cell lineâ€™s resistance to different chemotherapeutic drugs. The model is accessible through this website, where users can input their dataset, and receive the predictions. The model is trained and focused on four of the most common cancers in Malaysia, i.e, breast, lung, colorectal, and head & neck cancer. For each of the cancer cell line, the training dataset includes their multi-omics data, i.e, transcriptomics (gene expression), and proteomics (protein expression). Overall, the goal of our project is to contribute to oncology using data science, where this website serves a wide range of users, including oncologists, clinical researchers, biomedical scientists, and bioinformatics professionals. It supports efforts in personalized medicine by helping users identify potential non-responders in standard chemotherapy treatments.
